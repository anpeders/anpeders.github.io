About Me

Who I Am

Hello! My name is Andy Pederson. I live in Lincoln, Nebraska with my wife Courtney. We have three cats and a dog. My hobbies include video games, film, and playing bass guitar. After working as a bookseller and later as an assistant manager, I started at the Center for Digital Research in the Humanities in 2018. I decided I wanted to go back to school shortly after I began working there.

Education

I graduated from Lincoln Southwest High School in 2007. I received my Bachelor of Arts in English from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln in 2012. I applied to the University of Missouri-Columbia in 2019, then started this Master of Library and Information Science program in spring 2020. I plan on graduating in fall 2022.

I must not fear. Fear is the mind-killer. Fear is the little-death that brings total obliteration. I will face my fear. I will permit it to pass over me and through me. And when it has gone past I will turn the inner eye to see its path. Where the fear has gone there will be nothing. Only I will remain.
