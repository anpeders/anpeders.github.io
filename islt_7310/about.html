Who I Am

Hello! My name is Andy Pederson. I live in Lincoln, Nebraska with my wife Courtney. We have three cats and a dog. My hobbies include video games, film, and playing bass guitar. After working as a bookseller and later as an assistant manager at Zipline Brewing Company, I started at the Center for Digital Research in the Humanities in 2018. I decided I wanted to go back to school shortly after I began working there.
